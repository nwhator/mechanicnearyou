== Description ==
RomethemeKit for Elementor Page Builder is the best toolkit solution for Elementor, It includes a Header and Footer layout builder and also comes with 25+ Elementor addons, 100+ Elementor Templates KIT (Growing Each Week),  and premade widget blocks for Elementor websites. With RomethemeKit, build Elementor websites faster and easier than ever! New Elementor widgets and features will be uploaded and improved regularly.


FEATURES
• Build with Elementor
• Elementor Header Footer Builder
• Elementor Widget Ready To use
• Rometheme Block & Template Library
• Rometheme Icons Pack
• Expert Support Team

WHAT IS NEW?
• Line Chart - Show a graphical representation of data to see the analytics side of your workflow.

• Pie and Doughnut Chart - Create Customized and Visually Appealing Charts with Pie and Donut Chart Widget.

• Progress Bar - Highlight your skill stats with the help of Animated progress bars.

• Counter - An impressive widget that allows you to show important facts on your website.

• Blog Post - Display blog posts beautifully on your WordPress websites built through Elementor.

• Call To Action - Bring an image, button, content, and other features under one box with the call to action widget.

• Blockquote -  Important quotes or testimonials within an eye-catching quotation box

• Social Share - Social share widget gives a diverse way to make their clients comfort. Also, it helps the visitor to share their feelings about the site.

• Team - Show off your team of experts with this widget.

• Text Marquee - Horizontal scrolling text on your website for dynamic content presentation.

• Animated Heading - add animation in the heading of your content using this heading creator tool. 

• Card Slider - Display featured posts, announcements, latest posts, custom content etc.

• Accordion - Add beautiful nested accordions anywhere

• Testimonial Carousel - Show your clients feedback, and recommendations on your sites with a perfect testimonial widget. Like our other addons, it has various customizable options like layout, wrapper content style, description, title separator, client, etc.

• Bar Chart - Show a graphical representation of data to see the analytics side of your workflow.

• Advanced Tab - Display nested elements including text box, image box, icon box, contact form, MailChimp subscription form, FAQ, team, social feed, blog, and much more within the tabs.


HEADER & FOOTER WIDGETS
• Header Offcanvas - Header Offcanvas allow the user to display sidebar content like the demo and registration when the specific button is triggered.

• Nav Bar - Responsive Navbar options with lots of possibilities and modern design.

• Search - Search is a simple search widget that provides interactive search fields on your webpage for your users. You can get a simple search, modal options also.

• Site Logo

• Header Info - Display your logos, texts, business info, advertisements, and banners using our header addons.
